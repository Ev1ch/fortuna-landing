The first zero commission blockchain-based app for cryptocurrency and stock trading

Fortuna is the first commission-free, blockchain-based trading and investing app that is focused on user experience, providing superior conditions for traders, as well a offering a level of security and transparency up to the highest standards of technology available today.

Start trading crypto risk free for the first time

Fortuna offers a full-fledged experience to every trader regardless of their knowledge and experience. Beginners can start with a demo account and even win free Fortuna tokens to start trading for real.

The revolution in mobile trading and investing apps

We are planning on becoming the go-to app for crypto and stock trading that uses Smart Contracts for deposits and order executions.

Advanced analytics

Fortuna offers much more information and analysis tools to the users that are usually available on mobile trading apps today. We are implementing features that are only available on the top web-based applications and platforms.

Advanced charting

Fortuna offers advanced charting options such as candlesticks that are very rarely seen in mobile trading apps. The custom charting functionality will let the user customize the charts they want to see and display certain metrics.

Margin trading

We are offering margin trading with below-average interest rates compared to our competitors. Traders with higher ranks from the gamification system will get access to lower interest rates and higher margin ceilings.

Full transparency

Fortuna discloses information regarding the payment for order flow transparently on the blockchain. By doing so, users will have the exact information on what the difference in their buy or sell price is.

The Fortuna Utility token

The MVT token is the project’s native token that will be used as the main currency in the ecosystem. The token will be used by traders to pay fees such as interests on margin trading and subscriptions. Fortuna will further act as an incentive mechanism, rewarding traders for engaging with the learning resources provided on the platform, referring friends, reaching milestones and for other kinds of engagement with the ecosystem.
